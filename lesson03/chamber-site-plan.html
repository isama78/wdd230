Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Mauro Jose Isa

WDD 230

Overview

Site Name:

Cordoba Chamber of Commerce

I chose this name because of the city where the chamber is based.

Site Purpose

  • Provide a platform where local merchants can register their businesses, display their products or services and connect with the community, in order to foster economic growth in the region and strengthen local business identity.
  • Provide up-to-date information on business regulations, financing opportunities, business advice and training events.
  • The website will be the central point for registration for events whose purpose will be to promote networking between local businesspeople and facilitate the exchange of ideas and collaboration opportunities.

Scenarios

  • How can I register my business with the Chamber of Commerce and what are the benefits of doing so?
  • What events or training programs does the Chamber of Commerce offer to help my business grow?
  • What are the networking and collaboration opportunities available through the Chamber of Commerce?
  • What financial support or business advisory services does the Chamber of Commerce offer for local businesses?
